The U.S. Trade Landscape

Global View
Hover on the map to explore how much the U.S. exported to each country in total during the WTO period between 1995 and 2018 (the darker the country is, the more the U.S. exported to). Below the map is a bar graph, where you can use the year dropdown to see the top 10 countries that the U.S. exported to each year.
You can hover on the map to see the export values, and select the year you'd like to view in detail by clicking on the circle. The graph on the right will update accordingly.
This isotype graph gets updated based on your previous selections, which are also listed above (the default is Canada 2018). Explore with the selections on the left!
